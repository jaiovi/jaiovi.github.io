## Academy Award nominee

On August 24, 2021, the Ariel Award nominees were announced and the short film got nominated. Thanks to this, Sebastián Jaiovi began the journey that would eventually become Studio Kixta.

## Development

Short film made during the pandemic. Built around SpaceX concept of travelling to space.

Autodesk Maya technologies used:
- Rigging
- Scenes and light layers
- Xgen for grass generation
