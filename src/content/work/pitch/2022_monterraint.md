### Synopsis:

"Monterrain't" is the development bible for a 2D adventure-dramedy animated series targeting an audience of 7-11 years old. The story is set in an alternative Monterrey, Mexico valley, where the mushroom village faces a terrible drought. 

The crisis is instigated by a flock of obsessive turkeys, led by a pretentious enemy, who seeks to seize the scarce remaining water. 

The series follows the unexpected partnership between Baffy, a naive and sheltered raven, and Millie, a hyperactive mushroom girl. Together with Millie's older sister Leura, they embark on a quest to protect their town’s most valuable resource, the water, learning the importance of community and environmental care. 

### My Contribution

I was responsible for developing the overall story arc and the central conflict. Specifically, the concept of the town-wide drought was inspired by and developed from the real-life water crisis in Monterrey, Mexico.

### Co-Creator Zahira

Zahira, focused on story arc details, the graphics and visual style of the project.
